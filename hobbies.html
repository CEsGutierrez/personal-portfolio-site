food

I enjoy the process of learning new recipes, to me that could be finding alternative ingredients, how the product is supposed to look, smell and possibly taste during various stages of the process and refining my understanding of the procedure. In practice, it looks like I really love Punjabi Butter Chicken, which I do, but I made it a lot in the six-month period when I was learning how to lower the cook time, prep, and simplify the ingredients that needed to be on hand while maintaining the flavor and texture of the dish. The list is of some dishes that I’m working on now, or have worked on in the past, with recipes included when they were used as a jumping-off point. Enjoy.
